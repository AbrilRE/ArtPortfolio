Unreal

This proyect was developed on the last year of my career as final proyect for my Videogames assignature, coded on C# using Unity.

Poo Fights!

This proyect was developed on the thrid semester of my career as final proyect for the assignature Object Oriented Programming, coded in Java using Greenfoot.
Tells a short story of a student that lost her notebook the day of her final test, she has to clear levels where she can get lost pages of her notebook, at the end of the game she presents her final test with the help of the lost pages.
